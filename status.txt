Status
~~~~~~

[ GET ] /ships
  returns a collection of ships

[ POST ] /ships/{ShipId}
  returns a single ships

[ PATCH ] /ships/{ShipId}
  updates a ship by its ShipId

[ DELETE ] /ships/{ShipId}
  deletes a ship by its ShipId
